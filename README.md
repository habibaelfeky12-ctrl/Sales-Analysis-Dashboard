📈 Visual Insights

🌍 Top 10 Countries by Sales

Displays the top countries contributing to revenue.

Helps identify the most profitable markets geographically.

📅 Sales by Quarter

Shows the sales trend across different quarters.

Helps track seasonal performance and growth trends.

🗣 Sales by Language

Analyzes sales distribution based on language.

Arabic and English represent the largest share of sales.

🛍 Profit by Category

Shows which product categories generate the highest profits.

Helps businesses focus on high-performing product segments.

💰 Selling Price vs Cost

Compares the selling price and cost of different products.

Useful for identifying profit margins and pricing efficiency.

⭐ Category Rating Distribution

Displays the percentage distribution of different product categories.
